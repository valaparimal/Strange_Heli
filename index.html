🚁 Strange Heli - Game Description 🧟

Strange Heli is an action-packed helicopter game where you must stop zombies from reaching the sky!

🎮 How to Play:

✅ Control the Helicopter: Move left, right, forward, and backward to dodge and attack.

✅ Attack with Bombs: Press Spacebar to drop bombs.

✅ Zombie Movement: Zombies rise from the ground to the sky. Destroy them before they reach you!

🔥 Objective:

Destroy as many zombies as possible and survive as long as you can!

⚠ Game Over Condition:

If a zombie touches the helicopter, the game ends!

💣 Are you ready for the challenge?

Fly, fight, and bomb your way to victory! 🚁💥
